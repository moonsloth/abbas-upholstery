Professional and Affordable Upholstery Services

From living room furniture to restaraunt booths, Abbas Upholstery has your upholstery needs covered. With our wide selection of fabrics and other materials, we can help you bring your upholstery ideas to life on any budget.

What We Do

Residential Upholstery

Is your furniture worn down or damaged? Need to add support to your cushions? Maybe you want a custom couch? We can help with all of that and more! We offer free pick up and delivery in the San Diego Area along with free estimates with competitive prices.

Commercial Upholstery

We specialize in building custom boothes for restaraunts, barbershop seats, and more. Offering commercial grade material that lasts and passes fire code requirements.

Automobile Upholstery

We offer motorcycle seat repairs, car seat reupholsters, and headliner replacements.
